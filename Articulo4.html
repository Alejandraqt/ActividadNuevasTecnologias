Artículo #4

Effect of virtual reality therapy on pain relief in sports rehabilitation for young adults: A systematic review and meta-analysis

Autores: Fecha: Fuente:
  • Elyn Toh
  • Si Qi Yoong
  • Olivia Win Myint
  • Qian Ning Leong
  • Ying Jiang
 Mayo 2025 Annals of Physical and Rehabilitation Medicine

Resumen:

Virtual reality is an emerging technology that intends to promote motor and perceptual-cognitive skills, which could benefit people with sports injuries. However, there is a lack of meta-analyses on its effectiveness in sports injury rehabilitation for young adults. This systematic review and meta-analysis of randomised controlled trials evaluated the effectiveness of virtual reality therapy (VRT) on sports-related musculoskeletal rehabilitation in reducing pain and improving functional levels and balance among young adults.
